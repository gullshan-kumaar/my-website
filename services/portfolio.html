3D Animation & Advertising

Neon Cyberpunk City

Client

FutureTech Corp

Services

3D Modeling, VFX, Sound Design

Date

October 2023

Tools Used

Blender, Houdini, After Effects

The Challenge

FutureTech needed a high-energy visual campaign to launch their new gaming processor. They wanted to move away from standard hardware photography and dive into a fully immersive, 3D-generated world that represented the speed and power of their chip.

The Solution

We built a procedural cyberpunk city using Blender and Houdini. By utilizing volumetric lighting and high-fidelity textures, we created a 30-second spot that transitions from the microscopic details of the processor into a sprawling futuristic metropolis. The result was a 40% increase in pre-orders.

Print Adaptations

In addition to the video assets, we rendered high-resolution stills at 8K for large-format billboards and magazine spreads.
